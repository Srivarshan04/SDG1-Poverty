" Poverty in Africa"

Poverty in Africa is marked by low income, lack of basic services, and economic hardship. Factors like political instability, climate change, and weak infrastructure contribute to high unemployment, poor healthcare, and food insecurity. Despite rich resources, inequality persists, but efforts in governance, education, and development aim to reduce poverty and drive progress..
